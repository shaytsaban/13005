In this intensive summer program you’ll explore the core principles of modern financial management—from capital budgeting and risk assessment to valuation and strategic financing decisions. Each meeting blends theory with hands‑on exercises drawn from real‑world business cases, equipping you with the tools and confidence to make sound financial choices.
