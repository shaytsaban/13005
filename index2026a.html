About the Course

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the core principles and practices of financial management. We will explore:

  • Calculating Present Value (PV) and Future Value (FV)
  • Understanding and applying various interest rates
  • Real-world financial applications and problem-solving
  • Financial planning, budgeting, and loan analysis
  • Fundamentals of risk, return, and portfolio management
  • Strategies for effective capital allocation
